Hello, my name is Huy Le and welcome to my homepage!

 To learn a little about me is that, currently, I am working as a Chemist and am in the transition of becoming a Software Engineer. I made the decision to pursue an education in Computer Science before I have to begin settling down and adult. This decision to pursure this education sparked when one of my employers was looking into automating lab work to better utilize lab personel's time. We ended up finding an lab equipment manufacturer that was capable of building equipment based on our needs and that also meet our style, practices, or techniques. They even integrate their equipment to our instruments and wrap their software around the current software to make equipment compatible with each other. This was really cool to me. Once I was exposed to that, I really wanted to know how I could do that and this is where I picked up a goal of someday becoming a embedded software developer. I hope to contribute to the Scientific Industry by combining my skills as a chemist and a computer scientist one day.
